## MATERIALS NEEDED

1. **Cardstock Paper**
   - 110 lb (300 gsm) recommended
   - White or cream colored
   - Minimum 80 lb (216 gsm)

2. **Printer**
   - Color inkjet or laser
   - Print at highest quality
   - Borderless optional

3. **Cutting Tools**
   - Paper cutter (best)
   - X-Acto knife + ruler
   - Scissors (acceptable)

4. **Optional Enhancements**
   - Laminator + pouches
   - Card sleeves (standard trading card size)
   - Rounded corner punch

## PRINTING PROCESS

1. **Print artwork pages first**
   - Insert artwork files
   - Print at highest quality
   - Let ink dry 5 minutes

2. **Print card text**
   - Use template above
   - Match card names to artwork
   - Check alignment

3. **Cut cards**
   - Use paper cutter for straight edges
   - Cut 3 columns first
   - Then cut 3 rows
   - Results in 9 cards

4. **Optional: Laminate**
   - Use standard laminating pouches
   - Feed through laminator
   - Trim excess laminate
   - Prevents wear during gameplay

5. **Optional: Sleeve**
   - Use standard trading card sleeves
   - Protects without laminating
   - Easy to update/replace

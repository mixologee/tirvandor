## ğŸ¯ WHAT IS THE CAMPAIGN DASHBOARD?

The Campaign Dashboard is your **mission control** for running Tirvandor campaigns. It gives you a bird's-eye view of:

- **Party status** (levels, health, resources)
- **Quest progress** (active, completed, failed)
- **NPC relationships** (who likes/hates the party)
- **Faction standings** (political landscape)
- **Major plot threads** (what's happening in the world)
- **Timeline** (when things will happen)
- **Resources** (money, magic items, assets)

**Think of it as:** The cockpit dashboard of an airplane - everything important in one place!

## WHAT TO TRACK VS. WHAT TO SKIP:

**ALWAYS TRACK:**
- Party vital stats
- Active quests
- Major NPC relationships
- Faction standings
- Upcoming deadlines

**OPTIONAL:**
- Minor NPCs
- Completed quests (archive elsewhere)
- Exact gold amounts (round to nearest 10/100)
- Mundane items

**SKIP:**
- Every dice roll
- Exact dialogue
- Insignificant encounters
- Stuff players track themselves

## KEEP IT SIMPLE:

- Don't track everything
- Use shorthand
- Focus on what drives story
- Simplify as needed

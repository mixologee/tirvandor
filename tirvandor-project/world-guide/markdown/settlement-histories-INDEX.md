# Settlement Histories of Tirvandor

**Noble Houses, Power Struggles, and Centuries of Drama**

*Historical enrichment for major settlements connecting them to great houses and world events*

---

## About This Document

This document provides detailed histories for key settlements across Tirvandor, showing how noble houses founded, ruled, lost, and sometimes reclaimed these locations over 1,247 years.

**Note:** Due to the extensive length of the full settlement histories (35,000+ words), the complete content is available in the original source document. This index provides quick reference to what's covered.

---

## Settlements Documented

**Total:** 17 detailed settlements (with plans for 30 total)
- **Thaldros:** 10 settlements
- **Aethoria:** 5 settlements
- **Islands:** 2 settlements

Each entry includes:
- Foundation Story - Who founded it, when, and why
- House Connections - Which noble families have ruled or influenced it
- Major Historical Events - Sieges, disasters, transfers of power
- Current Status - Who rules now and political situation
- Historical Secrets - Mysteries tied to the settlement's past
- Adventure Hooks - Plot seeds emerging from history

## Thaldros - Western Continent

### Major Cities

1. **Kaer Thandros** (Capital City)
   - Population: ~45,000
   - Founded: 612 CR by King Thorin I Thandris
   - Key Features: Crown Forge, Royal Capital, mountain fortress
   - Current Ruler: King Aldric IV Thandris
   - Major Secrets: Crown Forge mystery, Dragon Treaty, The Deep Thing, Royal Bastards
   - Status: Royal capital, center of power

2. **Raven's Keep** (Ruins)
   - Former Population: ~8,000 (at height)
   - Founded: 587 CR by Lord Aldric Blackwood I
   - Fell: 1092 CR (Blackwood Rebellion)
   - Key Features: Haunted ruins, hidden vaults, supernatural activity
   - Major Secrets: Hidden family vaults, Fey pact, Lost heir, The Ravens
   - Status: Forbidden ruins, officially forbidden entry

3. **Silverpine** (Major City)
   - Population: ~14,000
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend (Ancient elven settlement)
   - House: Silverleaf (450 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: World-tree city, elven capital, forest magic
   - Current Rulers: Lord Elenion & Lady Silvara Silverleaf
   - Major Secrets: Starweaver correspondence, Blackwood survivors, Dying Heart Tree
   - Status: Seat of House Silverleaf, Forest Capital

4. **Goldreach** (Major City)
   - Population: ~16,000
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend as Deepwater port
   - House: Goldshore (698 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: Maritime capital, banking empire, largest western port
   - Current Ruler: Lord Aldris III Goldshore (half-elf, age 178)
   - Major Secrets: Black Fleet, Debt trap, Bastard war, Eastern conspiracy
   - Status: Maritime and financial powerhouse

5. **Crossvale** (Major City)
   - Population: ~18,000
   - Founded: 698 CR (planned city)
   - House: Heartwood (1000 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: Trade hub, five roads meet, grain capital
   - Current Ruler: Lord Gareth Heartwood
   - Major Secrets: Seven hidden caches, The Blight, Price-fixing cartel, Dark ritual
   - Status: Trade crossroads, agricultural center

6. **Sundara** (Major City)
   - Population: ~18,000
   - Founded: 967 CR by Malik Ashborne
   - House: Ashborne (1050 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: Desert capital, oasis city, exotic trade
   - Current Ruler: Sultan Rashid Ashborne
   - Major Secrets: Buried city, Water magic, Djinn pact, Succession murder
   - Status: Desert capital, trade monopoly

7. **Ashgate** (Major City)
   - Population: ~9,000
   - Founded: 920 CR by House Emberthorn
   - House: Ashborne (1220 CR - Present) / Was Emberthorn until dragon
   - Key Features: Volcanic trade, obsidian mining, gateway to barrens
   - Key Figure: Lady Sera Emberthorn (last of line, destitute)
   - Major Secrets: Dragon's return, Emberthorn vaults, The bargain, Hidden heir
   - Status: Recovering from dragon occupation

### Towns and Fortresses

8. **Ironhold** (Major Town)
   - Population: ~8,000
   - Founded: 612 CR (with Kaer Thandros)
   - Status: Military fortress, mountain pass defense
   - Loyalty: Unwavering to House Thandris
   - Major Secrets: Sealed pass, Deserter cache

9. **Deepforge** (Mining Town)
   - Population: ~3,500
   - Founded: 780 CR
   - House: Ironmark
   - Features: Deep mythril mines, volcanic forges
   - Ruler: Thane Borin VII Ironmark (age 287)
   - Major Secrets: Deep breach chamber, Stolen mythril

10. **Emberforge** (Town)
    - Population: ~2,400
    - Founded: 920 CR by House Emberthorn
    - Features: Volcanic forges, eternal heat
    - Status: Independent (mastersmith guild control)
    - Major Secrets: Dragon's forge creation

## Aethoria - Eastern Continent

### Major Cities

1. **Aethermere** (Capital City)
   - Population: ~38,000
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend (Ancient druidic site)
   - House: Aethermere (Year 0 - Present)
   - Key Features: Five world-trees, chosen-heir system, druidic capital
   - Current Rulers: Queen Silviana Aethermere (age 543), Heir Theron (half-elf, controversial)
   - Major Secrets: Dying world-tree, Rejected heir, Succession manipulation, Forest consciousness
   - Status: Royal capital of Aethoria

2. **Starfall** (Major City)
   - Population: ~6,500
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend (Observatory site)
   - House: Starweaver (450 CR - Present, formerly Starleaf)
   - Key Features: Grand Observatory, meteor strikes, astronomical research
   - Current Rulers: Archmagister Elaris Starweaver (age 612), Heir Lumina
   - Major Secrets: Sealed prophecy, Dark star observation, Greenstar Codex location, Stolen Stellar Crown
   - Status: Observatory city, magical academy

3. **Crystalbrook** (Major City)
   - Population: ~12,000
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend as Deepwater outpost
   - House: Crystalborn (450 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: Crystal towers, water-speaking magic, lake control
   - Current Ruler: Lady Aria Crystalborn (age 234, half-elf)
   - Major Secrets: Deep Voice entity, Marina's heir, Succession murders, Kraken alliance, War preparation
   - Status: Trade hub, water magic center

4. **Marshaven** (Major City)
   - Population: ~8,000
   - Founded: 850 CR by House Thornveil
   - House: Thornveil (850 CR - Present)
   - Key Features: Swamp city, alchemy capital, poison trade
   - Current Rulers: Lord Maric Thornveil (age 67), Heir Lyra (age 34, plotting coup)
   - Major Secrets: Plague Garden, Assassination bureau, Swamp pact, Coup plot, Controlled epidemic plan
   - Status: Alchemical center, growing power

5. **Wildgrove** (Sacred Site)
   - Population: ~5,000
   - Founded: Ancient (Pre-Worldrend druidic sanctuary)
   - Former House: Splittide (700-1092 CR, crushed in rebellion)
   - Current: Druid council, neutral ground
   - Key Features: World-tree grove, fey court, memorial to Splittide
   - Major Secrets: Splittide survivors in Feywild, Pending return, Land's anger
   - Status: Neutral druidic sanctuary

## Islands & Neutral Territories

1. **Port Myrthen** (Major City - Island)
   - Population: ~9,000
   - Founded: Pre-Worldrend (survived as island)
   - Government: Merchant Council (Seven Princes)
   - Key Features: Neutral ground, black market, spy center
   - Major Secrets: Hidden vault, Spy center, Ancient treaty, Underground city
   - Status: Independent neutral trade hub

2. **Palace Port** (Sunken City)
   - Former Population: ~50,000
   - Sank: Year 0 (The Worldrend)
   - Former House: Deepwater (headquarters)
   - Key Features: Sunken capital, royal palace, vast treasures
   - Depth: 600+ feet below surface
   - Major Secrets: Crystal Throne, Deepwater will, The Ledger, High King's Sword, Guardian entity
   - Status: Underwater ruins, target of salvage operations

## üéØ SESSION STRUCTURE MUSIC GUIDE

**Pre-Session (Players Arriving):**
- Light tavern music or peaceful ambience
- Sets the mood while people settle in

**Recap & Planning:**
- Minimal background music (20% volume)
- Match previous session's location

**Exploration / Travel:**
- Region-appropriate ambient
- Low intensity, allows conversation

**Social Encounters:**
- Location-appropriate music
- Even quieter, speech priority

**Rising Tension:**
- Shift to tense exploration tracks
- Increase volume slightly

**Combat:**
- Switch immediately to combat music
- Match difficulty to track intensity

**Post-Combat:**
- Victory music (brief)
- Then back to exploration/rest

**Emotional Moments:**
- Reduce to minimal ambience
- Let players' roleplay shine

**Cliffhanger / End:**
- Build to dramatic crescendo
- Cut to silence on perfect moment

---

## üí° ADVANCED TIPS

**Creating Tension:**
- Slowly reduce ambient sounds to silence
- Sudden silence = something is coming
- Single ominous drone note

**Combat Escalation:**
- Start with skirmish music
- Switch to battle when things get serious
- Boss music when BBEG appears

**Emotional Impact:**
- Reduce music during key dialogue
- Bring in emotional strings for big moments
- Silence for maximum dramatic weight

**Regional Consistency:**
- Keep location music consistent
- Players will associate sounds with places
- Creates strong mental geography

**Volume Control:**
- Music should NEVER overpower speech
- Ambient at 20-30% max
- Fade out for important NPC dialogue

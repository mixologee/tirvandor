## EQUIPMENT & STARTING GEAR

### BEYOND STANDARD STARTING EQUIPMENT
Iron Guild Members Get:
Guild tabard/insignia (marks you as professional)
Basic mercenary contract template
Access to Guild safe houses in major towns
Iron Guild signet ring (for identification)
Recommended Purchases:
Healing potions – You'll need them
Rope and climbing gear – Always useful
Thieves' tools – For those sneaky contracts
Good quality weapon – Your life depends on it
Armor upgrades – As soon as you can afford
Optional Equipment:
Disguise kit (covert ops)
Forgery kit (papers and passes)
Merchant's scale (verify payment)
Signet rings (fake identities)

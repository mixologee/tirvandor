## CONTRACT TYPES

### WHAT KINDS OF JOBS WILL YOU TAKE?
- COMBAT CONTRACTS (High Pay, High Risk)
- Caravan escort through bandit territory
- Assault on fortified position
- Monster extermination
- Bodyguard for VIP in war zone
- Siege warfare (offensive or defensive)
- COVERT CONTRACTS (Medium Pay, Medium Risk)
- Sabotage enemy supplies
- Assassination (be careful who you kill)
- Intelligence gathering
- Infiltration and theft
- Message delivery through hostile territory
- SUPPORT CONTRACTS (Medium Pay, Low Risk)
- Training local militia
- Fortification construction
- Supply line security
- Diplomatic escort
- Base defense
- SHADY CONTRACTS (High Pay, Reputation Risk)
- Working for criminals
- Morally questionable targets
- Contracts that violate laws
- Jobs from untrustworthy clients
- Missions with hidden agendas
- SPECIAL CONTRACTS (Variable)
- Guild internal missions
- Rescue operations
- Artifact recovery
- Investigation work
Long-term employment

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Q: Can I be a good person in this campaign?
A: Yes! Many mercenaries have moral codes. You choose which contracts to take.
Q: Will we be forced to do evil things?
A: No. You can refuse any contract. But refusing loses potential income and some contracts may test your morals.
Q: Can we switch sides during a contract?
A: Technically yes, but expect severe reputation consequences. The Guild Code forbids it.
Q: What if my character wouldn't take a contract?
A: Then don't! But have an in-character reason and accept the consequences.
Q: Can we work for both Kingdom forces and frontier territories?
A: Absolutely. The Guild is neutral. Your only loyalty is to gold and the current contract.
Q: How lethal is this campaign?
A: Moderately lethal. Combat is dangerous but healers are available. Smart play keeps you alive.
Q: Can I play a pacifist?
A: Very difficult in a mercenary campaign. Consider a support role (healer, face) instead.
Q: What if we want to eventually pick a side?
A: That's an option! The campaign can evolve based on your choices.

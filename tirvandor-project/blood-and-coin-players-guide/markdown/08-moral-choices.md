## STARTING THE CAMPAIGN

### SESSION ZERO TOPICS
Discuss as a group:
1. Lines and Veils
What content is off-limits? (torture, SA, etc.)
What should fade to black?
What are you comfortable with?
2. Party Composition
Do we have balanced roles?
Who's the face for contract negotiations?
Who's the tactical leader in combat?
3. Squad Dynamics
How do we know each other?
Do we trust each other?
What's our squad name?
4. Moral Boundaries
What contracts will we refuse?
Are we "noble" mercenaries?
Where do we draw the line?
5. Goals
Short-term: What's our first goal?
Long-term: What do we want to achieve?
Personal: Individual character goals?

### STARTING SCENARIO OPTIONS
OPTION A: THE ROOKIE CONTRACT
You're fresh recruits on first real mission
Simple escort contract
Things go wrong (they always do)
Prove yourselves to the Guild
OPTION B: THE REUNION
You're veterans reuniting after months apart
Each had different contracts
New mission brings you back together
Jump right into action
OPTION C: THE TRIAL
You're accused of breaking the Guild Code
Must complete impossible mission to prove innocence
High stakes from the start
Establishes squad bond through adversity
DM will choose or let group vote

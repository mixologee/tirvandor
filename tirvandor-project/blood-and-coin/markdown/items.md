# Act I Items

## Quest 1.1: The Consortium Job

### **Elise'S Glamoured Leather**
*Armor (leather), uncommon*

**AC:** 11 + Dex modifier  
**Attunement:** No

**Properties:**

**Value:** 350gp

## Quest 1.2: The Hunters

### **Kael'S Poisoned Blades**
*Weapon (shortsword), rare (requires attunement)*

**Damage:** 1d6 + Dex piercing  
**Properties:** Finesse, light, special

**Venom Reservoir:** Blade has 3 charges. As bonus action, expend 1 charge to coat blade with poison (lasts 1 minute). Next hit: DC 15 Con save, 3d6 poison damage (half on success). Regains 1d3 charges at dawn.

**Lore:** Kael's signature weapons. Each blade has hollow core containing concentrated venom.

**Location:** Wielded by Kael Shadowbane

**Value:** 2,500gp

---

### **Ring OF Shadows**
*Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)*

**Properties:**
- +2 to Stealth checks
- Once per short rest: Cast *pass without trace* (self only)
- Shadows seem slightly deeper around wearer

**Lore:** Black Chains standard issue for senior members.

**Location:** Found in Black Chains treasure

**Value:** 750gp

## Quest 3.4: The Unification

### **The DREAD CROWN** (Phylactery)
*Artifact, artifact (cursed)*

**Do Not Wear**

**Properties:**
- +4 to Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma
- Can cast *detect thoughts* at will
- Can cast *dominate person* (DC 19, 3/day)
- **Immortality:** Wearer does not age

**Curse:** Wearing crown begins transformation into lich. Takes 1 year. Process is irreversible after 1 month. Wearer's alignment shifts to evil.

**Special:** This IS The Dread King's phylactery. While intact, he reforms after death. Must be destroyed to kill him permanently.

**Destruction:** Can only be destroyed by:
1. Dealing 100 damage (resist all but radiant/force)
2. Casting *disintegrate* successfully
3. Throwing into volcano or similar

**Lore:** The Dread King's crown IS his phylactery. He wears his own soul-prison, ensuring it's always with him.

**Location:** On The Dread King's head, even when "dead"

**Value:** Priceless but too dangerous to use

**DM Note:** If PCs wear this, it's a campaign-ending mistake. Warn them clearly.

## DM Notes

### **Item Balance:**

All items are within standard D&D 5e power levels for their rarity. Nothing game-breaking except intentionally dangerous artifacts.

- **World-Splitter Components:** Campaign plot items, extremely dangerous
- **The Dread Crown:** Will turn PC into lich, warn players clearly
- **Dread King's Staff:** Cursed, but usable with caution

### **Treasure Philosophy:**

Items support campaign themes:
- Act I: Survival tools
- Act II: Power symbols
- Act III: Legend-tier rewards

# DM Notes

### **Monster Usage Guidelines:**

**Acts I-II:** Mix of humanoid enemies (guards, cultists, mercenaries)  
**Act II Finale:** Large-scale armies abstracted  
**Act III:** Mix of continued humanoids + undead threat  

### **Scaling Encounters:**

- **Low levels (1-5):** Use guards, cultists, bandits in groups of 4-8
- **Mid levels (6-11):** Knights, veterans, fanatics in groups of 2-6
- **High levels (11-15):** Elite enemies, undead hordes, boss monsters

### **Moral Complexity:**

Many "enemies" aren't evil:
- War refugees are innocents
- Aethoria militia are fighting for freedom
- Thornwood rangers are freedom fighters
- Some cultists are desperate/misled

**Don't make all combats simple good vs evil**

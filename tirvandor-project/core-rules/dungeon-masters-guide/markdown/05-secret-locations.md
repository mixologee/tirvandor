# Secret Locations
## Hidden Places of Power

Locations players won't find in the World Guide.

---

## The Black Archive
Location: Beneath Silverpine University
Secret Level: Tier 3 (Faculty only)

Description: A forbidden library containing pre-Sundering texts. Access requires 3 faculty keys.

Contents:
- True history of the Primordials
- Sealed tome: *The Binding Rituals*
- Map to 4 remaining Primordial prisons
- Research notes from Circle of Seven

Guardians: Constructs keyed to faculty bloodlines. 2 Shield Guardians, 4 Animated Armors.

Hook: PCs find 1 faculty key, must acquire others or break in.

---

## The Forgotten Monastery
Location: Ironspine Mountains, unmarked
Secret Level: Tier 2 (Rumored)

Description: Monks who worship The Forgotten One (missing Primordial). Believe it will return and bring enlightenment.

Features:
- 20 monks (CR 2 each, leader is CR 8)
- Library of erased histories
- Ritual chamber with time distortion effects
- Portal to a demiplane (locked)

Plot: Monks aren't evil—they genuinely seek knowledge. But their patron may be corrupted.

Treasure: *Staff of Forgotten Moments* (legendary), historical texts worth 5,000 gp.

---

## Nexus of Seven Sorrows
Location: Sundering Sea floor, exact coordinates lost
Secret Level: Tier 4 (Mythical)

Description: Where the first seal was broken. Now a corrupted ley nexus radiating void energy.

Hazards:
- Water breathing required
- Pressure: DC 15 Con save/hour or 1 exhaustion
- Void corruption: DC 18 Wis save when casting or spell warps unpredictably
- Haunted by drowned empire ghosts (10d10 specters)

Reward: Access to *The Seal Chamber*—can repair seal or drain power (dangerous).

Campaign Use: Final arc location for preventing second Sundering.

---

## The Wandering Tower
Location: Appears randomly near ley nexuses
Secret Level: Tier 3 (Known only to Circle survivors)

Description: Tower of the last surviving Circle of Seven member. Teleports to escape pursuit.

Occupant: Archmagus Valdren (archmage, 150 years old, guilt-ridden)

Interior:
- 7 floors, extradimensional spaces
- Library with Primordial research
- Scrying chamber tracking seal integrity
- Valdren's quarters (lonely, paranoid)

Encounter: Valdren attacks intruders initially, but can be convinced to share knowledge (DC 20 Persuasion, or prove you're trying to fix his mistakes).

Information: Complete history, ritual to reinforce seals (requires legendary quest), locations of all nexuses.

---

## Ashgate Undercity
Location: Beneath Ashgate
Secret Level: Tier 1 (Local rumor)

Description: Pre-Sundering ruins beneath the city. Government denies existence.

Levels:
- Basement Level: Smuggler tunnels, black market
- Ancient Sewers: Abandoned 200 years ago, now monster lair
- Imperial Ruins: Original city, partially flooded
- The Deep Vault: Where city's portion of imperial treasury was stored

Inhabitants:
- Smugglers (levels 1-2)
- Otyughs, ropers (levels 3-5)
- Undead imperial guards (levels 6-8)
- The Vault Guardian: Modified iron golem (CR 16)

Treasure: 50,000 gp in old imperial currency (collectors pay 3× face value), magic items, historical artifacts.

Hook: City council will pay 5,000 gp to clear undercity and recover treasury.

---

## The Singing Stones
Location: Northern Thaldros, remote valley
Secret Level: Tier 2 (Druid secret)

Description: Circle of standing stones that hum with ley line energy. Druids use for rituals.

Powers:
- Scrying across ley line network (requires attunement)
- Speak with ancient spirits (once per week)
- Portal to Feywild (new moon only)
- Ritual site (+5 to spellcasting checks here)

Guardians: Circle of druids (12 members, levels 5-9). Not hostile but protective.

Bargain: PCs can use stones if they complete quest for druids (usually ecological: stop loggers, cleanse corrupted nexus, etc.).

---

## The Obsidian Spire
Location: Border between Thaldros and Aethoria
Secret Level: Tier 4 (Legend)

Description: Tower of pure obsidian that appeared after the Sundering. No entrance visible.

Truth: Prison for The Devourer (Primordial of entropy). The spire IS the prison.

Approach: Requires flying or climbing DC 25. Touching spire: DC 20 Wis save or gain temporary madness.

Entry: Invisible door at apex, opens only to those carrying a Primordial artifact.

Interior:
- Single massive chamber
- The Devourer in center (size: gargantuan, appears as living void)
- Seal mechanisms in cardinal directions
- Each mechanism requires legendary artifact to operate

Encounter: The Devourer will bargain—offers incredible power in exchange for weakening just one seal mechanism. Lying. Will consume whoever accepts.

Outcome:
- Reinforce seal: Prevents catastrophe for 1,000 years
- Weaken seal: Devourer escapes in 1d10 years
- Drain power: Gain artifact but weaken seal by 50 years

## Goldreach Vault 13
Location: Beneath Goldreach central bank
Secret Level: Tier 1 (Known to thieves)

Description: Goldreach Bank has 12 public vaults. Vault 13 is unacknowledged.

Contents:
- Embezzled funds: 100,000 gp
- Blackmail material on 20 nobles
- Stolen art worth 50,000 gp
- Ledger proving bank owner funds Thornwood Syndicate

Security:
- Bank guards (20, levels 1-3)
- Magical locks (DC 25 to pick)
- Glyph of warding (fireball)
- Alarm spell (silent, alerts owner)

Heist Challenge: Getting in isn't hard—getting out with proof before guards arrive is.

Consequence: Exposing vault brings down bank owner, destabilizes Goldreach economy, but weakens Thornwood Syndicate.

## Temple of the Void Saints
Location: Contested Lands, moving camp
Secret Level: Tier 2 (Active threat)

Description: Cult worshipping The Devourer. Believe void will bring transcendence.

Leader: High Voidcaller Malthus (CR 11 warlock)

Activities:
- Raiding settlements for sacrifices
- Seeking Primordial artifacts
- Attempting to weaken seals
- Recruiting desperate refugees

Camp: 40 cultists (CR 1/2 each), 6 void-touched (CR 3), Malthus, portable altar (desecrated ground effect).

Plot: Malthus genuinely believes void will save people from suffering. Debate him (DC 22 Persuasion) or fight.

Loot: *Void Crystal* (arcane focus, +2 to spell attacks, deals 1d6 necrotic to wielder on critical fails), 8,000 gp stolen goods.

---

## The Riftmaw
Location: Exact position unknown, Sundering Sea
Secret Level: Tier 4 (Sailor legend)

Description: Massive wound in reality where two Primordials escaped. Raw planar chaos.

Hazards:
- Ships pulled toward it (DC 20 group check to escape)
- Planar effects: Roll on Wild Magic table every round
- Elementals pour through (endless spawn)
- Reality unstable: Gravity may reverse, time may slow

Opportunity: Portal to Elemental Planes, access to primordial essence (material component for epic magic).

Survival: DC 25 Arcana check to stabilize personal reality bubble. Lasts 10 minutes.

Treasure: Primordial essence (worth 10,000 gp per vial, 1d4 vials available), planar gems (5d10 × 100 gp).

## Using Secret Locations

Tier 1 (Levels 1-4): Ashgate Undercity, Goldreach Vault 13
Tier 2 (Levels 5-10): Forgotten Monastery, Singing Stones, Void Saints Temple
Tier 3 (Levels 11-16): Black Archive, Wandering Tower
Tier 4 (Levels 17-20): Nexus of Seven Sorrows, Obsidian Spire, Riftmaw

Integration: Drop hints in early game. Map fragments, NPC rumors, historical texts. Build toward high-level locations.

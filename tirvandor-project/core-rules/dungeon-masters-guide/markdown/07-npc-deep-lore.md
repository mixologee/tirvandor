# NPC Deep Lore
## Complete Stat Blocks for All Tirvandor NPCs

All non-campaign NPCs with full D&D 5e stat blocks, traits, actions, and roleplaying notes.

---

## Organization

NPCs are organized by category:
- Rulers and Nobility
- Military Leaders
- Arcane Authorities
- Religious Leaders
- Rogues and Criminals
- Warriors and Fighters
- Common Folk
- Special (Dragons, Historical, Divine)

## Rulers and Nobility (High CR)


### Grand Duke Valentin Rothgar

Medium humanoid (human), lawful neutral

Armor Class 17
Hit Points 112 (16d8 + 32)
Speed 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 15 (+2) | 18 (+4) |

Saving Throws Wis +6, Cha +8
Skills History +7, Insight +6, Persuasion +8
Senses passive Perception 15
Languages Common, plus 3 others
Challenge 10 (5,900 XP)
Proficiency Bonus +4

#### Traits

Royal Authority. Advantage on Charisma checks when dealing with subjects.

Tactical Mind. Can use bonus action to grant ally within 30 ft advantage on next attack.

#### Actions

Multiattack. Makes two rapier attacks or three dagger attacks.

Rapier +1. *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage.

Command (Recharge 5-6). One target within 60 ft must make DC 16 Wisdom save or follow one-word command on its next turn.

#### Reactions

Parry. Adds +3 AC against one melee attack that would hit.

#### Legendary Actions

Can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at end of another creature's turn. Regains spent legendary actions at start of turn.

Detect. Makes Wisdom (Perception) or Intelligence (Investigation) check.

Move. Moves up to speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Inspire (Costs 2 Actions). One ally within 60 ft gains advantage on next attack roll, saving throw, or ability check.

#### Roleplaying Notes

- Speaks with authority and expects obedience
- Weighs every word carefully for political impact
- Burdened by responsibility and secret knowledge

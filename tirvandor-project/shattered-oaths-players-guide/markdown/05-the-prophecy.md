## THE PROPHECY

### THE SHATTERED OATH
An ancient prophecy, found in the Temple of Moira the Weaver:
> "When crowns grow heavy with the weight of chains,
> When oaths are broken and freedom wanes,
> Seven shall rise where seven once fell,
> To break the cycle, to ring freedom's bell.
>
> Warrior, Scholar, Healer, Rogue,
> Mystic, Guardian, and Voice of the Folk,
> United by purpose, divided by past,
> The Shattered Oath mended at last.
>
> When the Iron Crown cracks at last,
> When the old order's chains are cast,
> Seven souls shall light the way,
> And freedom's dawn shall break the day."
What This Means:
Some believe you're the "Seven" from the prophecy
Thaldros fears this prophecy (they hunt "prophesied ones")
Aethoria sees it as hope
Whether it's real or self-fulfilling is up to you
Prophecy gives you narrative importance but not invincibility

## THE PROPHECY SYSTEM (Optional)

### FATE POINTS
The DM may use Fate Points to represent destiny:
Earning Fate Points:
Fulfill part of the prophecy
Heroic sacrifice
Major victory against tyranny
Inspire hope in others
Achieve character arc milestone
Spending Fate Points:
Reroll any d20 (after seeing result)
Turn a death into a dramatic survival
Have an NPC arrive at perfect moment
Gain sudden insight or revelation
Automatically succeed at crucial moment
Starting Fate: 1 point each

## THE RESISTANCE

### THE AETHORIAN FREEDOM MOVEMENT
You're likely part of (or allied with) the Resistance:
The Movement:
Decentralized network of cells
United by opposition to Thaldros
Mix of idealists, refugees, and fighters
Supported by Aethoria government (covertly)
Uses symbols: broken chain, rising sun
Resistance Activities:
Freeing slaves and prisoners
Sabotaging Thaldros operations
Protecting refugees
Gathering intelligence
Spreading hope and propaganda
Your Role:
Special operatives (you're heroes!)
Handle big threats and opportunities
Symbol for the cause
Bridge between cells

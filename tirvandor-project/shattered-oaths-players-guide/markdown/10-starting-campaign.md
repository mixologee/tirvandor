## STARTING THE CAMPAIGN

### SESSION ZERO TOPICS
Discuss as a group:
1. Heroism Level
How heroic are we?
Will we kill?
Where are our moral lines?
2. Prophecy Belief
Do our characters believe?
Is it real or inspiration?
How seriously do we take it?
3. Tone Preferences
How dark can it get?
Romance subplots?
Character death possible?
4. Party Dynamics
How do we know each other?
Who's the leader?
Any internal conflicts?
5. Personal Goals
Beyond "defeat Thaldros"
Character arcs and growth
What do you want to explore?

### STARTING SCENARIO OPTIONS
OPTION A: THE PROPHECY CALLS
You're gathered by a prophet/scholar
Told you're the "Seven" from prophecy
Given first mission
Prophecy-focused start
OPTION B: THE PRISON BREAK
You're escaping a Thaldros prison
Don't know each other yet
Survival bonds you
Action-heavy start
OPTION C: THE VILLAGE
You're in village raided by Thaldros
Must defend innocents together
Strangers becoming heroes
Emotional, immediate start
OPTION D: THE RESISTANCE
You're experienced rebels
New major mission
Already a team
Jump into action
DM will choose or let group vote

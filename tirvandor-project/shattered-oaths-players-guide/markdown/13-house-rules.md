## HOUSE RULES & CAMPAIGN MECHANICS

### SPECIAL RULES FOR SHATTERED OATHS
- **1. Heroic Inspiration**
- More frequent than normal
- For heroic acts and cool moments
- Can be stockpiled (max 3)
Share with allies
- **2. Bonds & Ideals**
- Your Bonds/Ideals matter mechanically
- Advantage when acting according to them
Inspiration for great roleplay
- **3. Death & Resurrection**
- Death is possible but rare
- Resurrection magic available (with cost)
- Heroic deaths may be permanent but meaningful
Discuss with DM before character death
- **4. Prophecy Moments**
- DM may invoke "prophecy moment"
- Dramatic scene where you shine
- Temporary power boost
Story significance
- 5. HOPE SYSTEM (Optional)
- Track "Hope" as party resource
- Inspire NPCs with Hope
- Spend Hope for divine aid
Lose Hope from defeats/betrayals

## TIPS FOR PLAYERS

### HOW TO BE A HERO
**In Combat:**
✅ Protect the weak
✅ Work as a team
✅ Dramatic moments over optimization
✅ Let everyone have spotlight
- ✅ Epic descriptions welcome
**Out Of Combat:**
✅ Develop your character
✅ Build relationships with NPCs
✅ Engage with the prophecy
✅ Make hard choices in character
- ✅ Have personal goals beyond main plot
**Roleplay:**
✅ Have flaws and struggles
✅ Let others shine too
✅ Build party bonds
✅ Engage with moral questions
- ✅ Let character grow and change
**Don't:**
❌ Be edgy loner - You're heroes together
❌ Murder everyone - You're better than that
❌ Ignore prophecy entirely - It's central to campaign
❌ Hog spotlight - Share the heroism
❌ Betray the party - This isn't that campaign

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Q: Do I have to believe in the prophecy?
A: No! Skeptical characters are valid. It's about the journey.
Q: Can we lose?
A: Yes, but not easily. Heroic stories have stakes.
Q: What if my character dies?
A: We'll discuss. Resurrection possible, or heroic sacrifice, or new character.
Q: Can we be dark and edgy?
A: Some darkness is fine, but remember the tone. You're heroes, not antiheroes.
Q: Will romance be in the campaign?
A: If you want! NPC romances are possible. Inter-party romance is up to you (communicate!).
Q: Can I be from Thaldros?
A: Yes! Defectors are great. You know the enemy intimately.
Q: How powerful will we become?
A: Very. By level 15, you're legendary heroes.
Q: Is the prophecy railroading?
A: No. It's a framework, not a script. You have choices.
Q: What if we disagree on morality?
A: Discuss in-character. Heroes can disagree and grow.
Q: Can we change the world?
A: That's the point! Your actions matter greatly.

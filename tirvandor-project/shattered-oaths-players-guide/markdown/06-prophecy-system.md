## THE PROPHECY SYSTEM (Optional)

### FATE POINTS
The DM may use Fate Points to represent destiny:
Earning Fate Points:
Fulfill part of the prophecy
Heroic sacrifice
Major victory against tyranny
Inspire hope in others
Achieve character arc milestone
Spending Fate Points:
Reroll any d20 (after seeing result)
Turn a death into a dramatic survival
Have an NPC arrive at perfect moment
Gain sudden insight or revelation
Automatically succeed at crucial moment
Starting Fate: 1 point each

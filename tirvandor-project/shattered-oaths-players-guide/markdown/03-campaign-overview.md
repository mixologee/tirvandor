## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

### WHAT IS SHATTERED OATHS?
Shattered Oaths is an epic fantasy campaign about heroes rising to fight tyranny, fulfill an ancient prophecy, and potentially reshape the fate of nations. You play freedom fighters, chosen heroes, or those thrust into greatness, standing against the oppressive might of the Thaldros Empire and championing the cause of liberty.
Core Themes:
Heroism and destiny
Freedom vs. tyranny
Ancient prophecy and fate
Sacrifice and duty
Hope in dark times
Found family and bonds
Campaign Tone:
Epic and inspirational
Morally clear (mostly)
Character-driven drama
High stakes and importance
Medium-high combat, high roleplay, medium exploration

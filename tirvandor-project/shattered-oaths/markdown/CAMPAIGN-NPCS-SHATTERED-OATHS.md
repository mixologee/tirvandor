## ALLIES & MENTORS

### **HIGH MAGE ARCTURUS**
*Medium humanoid (human), lawful good*

**Role:** Mentor, quest giver, powerful ally  
**Challenge:** 12 (8,400 XP)  
**Location:** Mage's Tower, travels with party

**Armor Class:** 12 (15 with *mage armor*)  
**Hit Points:** 99 (18d8 + 18)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 12 (+1)  
**INT** 20 (+5) | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saving Throws:** Int +9, Wis +7  
**Skills:** Arcana +13, History +13, Investigation +9  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 13  
**Languages:** Common, Draconic, Elvish, Celestial, Abyssal

**Archmage:** Can cast one 6th-level spell without expending a spell slot (1/day)

**Spellcasting (18th-level Wizard):**
- Cantrips (at will): *fire bolt, mage hand, prestidigitation, ray of frost*
- 1st level (at will): *detect magic, mage armor, magic missile, shield*
- 2nd level: *mirror image, misty step, web*
- 3rd level: *counterspell, fireball, fly*
- 4th level: *banishment, ice storm*
- 5th level: *cone of cold, wall of force*
- 6th level: *chain lightning, disintegrate*
- 7th level: *delayed blast fireball, teleport*
- 8th level: *mind blank, power word stun*
- 9th level: *time stop*, *wish* (rare emergency use)

**Actions:**
- **Staff:** +4 to hit, 1d6 bludgeoning  
- **Fire Bolt:** +9 to hit, 120 ft, 4d10 fire damage

**Personality:**
- Wise, patient mentor figure
- Believes in heroism and sacrifice
- Will die to save party if needed
- Provides guidance without controlling
- Represents "good wizards" archetype

**Key Scenes:**
- Initial recruitment and mentorship
- Provides critical information
- Final battle support
- Potential sacrifice moment

## ANTAGONISTS

### **LORD SHADOWS (CORVUS BLACKWOOD) - Final Boss**
*Medium humanoid (human), neutral evil*

**Role:** Main antagonist, final boss, Unbound leader  
**Challenge:** 15 (13,000 XP)  
**Location:** Final confrontation

**Armor Class:** 17 (bracers of defense)  
**Hit Points:** 180 (24d8 + 72)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 16 (+3) | **CON** 16 (+3)  
**INT** 22 (+6) | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 18 (+4)

**Saving Throws:** Int +11, Wis +8, Cha +9  
**Skills:** Arcana +16, Deception +9, History +16, Insight +8  
**Damage Resistances:** psychic  
**Damage Immunities:** poison  
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened  
**Senses:** truesight 60 ft., Passive Perception 13  
**Languages:** All

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day):** Choose to succeed on failed save

**Reality Warping (Components):** Possesses World-Splitter Components

**Spellcasting (20th-level Wizard):**
- Full spell list (see Shattered Oaths complete campaign)
- Can cast *wish* (1/long rest)
- Reality manipulation abilities

**Actions:**
- **Multiattack:** Three reality beam attacks
- **Reality Beam:** +11 to hit, 120 ft, 6d8 force damage
- **Power Word Kill:** If target has <100 HP, they die
- **Time Stop:** Takes 3 turns alone

**Legendary Actions (3/round):**
- Cast cantrip
- Reality Warp: Change terrain/environment
- Summon Shadows (Costs 2): 2d4 shadow creatures
- Cast Spell (Costs 3): Cast spell using slot

**Phases:**
- Phase 1: Testing heroes (uses lower-level spells)
- Phase 2: Serious (uses powerful magic)
- Phase 3: Desperate (goes all-out, uses wish)

**Personality:**
- Believes he's saving world
- Tragic villain
- Respects worthy opponents
- Will explain his plan (classic villain)
